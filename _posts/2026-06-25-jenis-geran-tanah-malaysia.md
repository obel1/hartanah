## Freehold vs Leasehold

**Geran (freehold)** means you own the land indefinitely. **Pajakan (leasehold)** means the state owns the land and you hold it for a fixed term, commonly 99 years, after which it must be renewed with a premium.

## Restrictions in interest

Some titles carry conditions such as *Rizab Melayu* (Malay Reserve) or low-cost restrictions. These limit who can buy and directly affect resale value and financing.

## Where to check

You can do a land search at the relevant Pejabat Tanah or through e-Tanah portals for most states. Always do this before signing any Sale and Purchase Agreement.
